Chapter 5: Arithmetic Progressions

Class 10 Mathematics Updated: May 15, 2025

Introduction

An Arithmetic Progression (AP) is a sequence where each term after the first is obtained by adding a fixed number (called the common difference) to the preceding term. This chapter explores the properties of APs, methods to find different terms, and sums of terms in an AP. The concepts learned here have wide applications in mathematics and real-world scenarios.

Key Concepts

Arithmetic Progression (AP)

  • Definition:
    • A sequence where each term after the first is obtained by adding a fixed number
    • The fixed number is called the common difference (d)
    • Example: 2, 5, 8, 11, 14, ... (d = 3)
  • First term (a) and Common difference (d):
    • First term is denoted by a
    • d = any term - previous term
    • d remains constant throughout the AP

nth Term of an AP

  • General Term Formula:
    aₙ = a + (n-1)d
    where:
    • aₙ is the nth term
    • a is the first term
    • n is the position of the term
    • d is the common difference
  • Finding n when aₙ is given:
    n = [(aₙ - a)/d] + 1

Sum of n Terms of an AP

  • Sum Formula:
    Sₙ = (n/2)[2a + (n-1)d]
    or
    Sₙ = (n/2)(first term + last term)
  • Arithmetic Mean:
    • AM = (Sum of terms)/(Number of terms)
    • AM of a and b = (a + b)/2

Important Questions and Solutions

Question 1

Find the 20th term of the AP: 3, 7, 11, 15, ...

Solution:

Given AP: 3, 7, 11, 15, ...

  1. First term (a) = 3
  2. Common difference (d) = 7 - 3 = 4
  3. We need to find a₂₀ (n = 20)
  4. Using the formula aₙ = a + (n-1)d:
    a₂₀ = 3 + (20-1)4
    = 3 + 19 × 4
    = 3 + 76
    = 79

Therefore, the 20th term is 79

Question 2

Find the sum of first 15 terms of the AP: 6, 13, 20, 27, ...

Solution:

Given AP: 6, 13, 20, 27, ...

  1. First term (a) = 6
  2. Common difference (d) = 13 - 6 = 7
  3. We need to find S₁₅ (n = 15)
  4. Using the formula Sₙ = (n/2)[2a + (n-1)d]:
    S₁₅ = (15/2)[2(6) + (15-1)7]
    = (15/2)[12 + 98]
    = (15/2)[110]
    = 15 × 55
    = 825

Therefore, the sum of first 15 terms is 825
